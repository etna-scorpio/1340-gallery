About

Why 1340Gallery?

1340gallery was born out of frustration. With the1340art magazine we want to give upcoming artist around the world a platform to express themselves and to show their work to a broader audience. We come across a lot of very talented artists. Unfortunately selling art on a larger scale or even globally can be difficult for any upcoming artist. "No Unsolicited Submissions", is a response, which many artists receive when they reach out to an art gallery or museum to introduce their work. 1340gallery is here to make a change in this arena. We want to make the pieces of art of the best and the most talented artists available for the public and the world.

Can anyone sell on 1340Gallery?

1340gallery supports the work of all emerging artists. However, at this point, registration is on invite only. Later, our platform will be open for all artists above 18 years of age.

We review applications on a weekly basis and you will be contacted by a member of the team following our next Application Review. Registration on invite is free of charge. If accepted for presentation, you will have to read and agree to our Terms and Conditions of presentation before your profile is made live online or before we can start selling your work.

Curated

Even though 1340art will eventually be open to artist all over the world, we want to maintain a certain level of standard. Our team of curators will evaluate every artists and their work indivudually. Only works accepted by our team of curators will be featured on our platform.

What do I need to do to start selling my artwork on 1340Gallery?

As stated earlier, 1340gallery is currently on invite only. If you are invited, please use our form to upload your artwork. Also, don’t forget to take some time to fill in your profile with information about yourself, such as where you studied, your exhibition history, and an artist’s statement. This kind of information can increase your chances of selling work and being noticed by our curators.

What can I sell on 1340gallery?

1340gallery provides a platform for artists to sell works in a variety of mediums, including: painting, collage, photography, drawing, sculpture, printmaking, mixed media, limited edition prints, installation, and open edition prints.

No Commission

If you sell an original artwork, 1340Gallery wont take any commission on the final sale price. We believe that the artists has the right to keep all profits from their work.

1340gallery is a platform designed specifically to support artists by giving them 100% of the proceeds from the sale of their artworks.

For more information please contact our support team at info@1340gallery.com
